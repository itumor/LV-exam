# Master Prompt for Another LLM: Generate 10 Latvian A2 Mock Exams

You are an expert exam designer for **Latvian State Language Proficiency** at **A2 level**.

Your task is to create **10 original mock exams** that follow the structure, skill balance, task types, scoring logic, and difficulty profile of the official **Valsts valodas prasmes pārbaude, pamata (A2) līmenis**.

## Main goal

Create **10 complete mock exams** with **different topics**, while keeping the **same exam structure** and **A2-level difficulty** throughout.

## Critical requirements

1. **Follow the exam structure exactly.**
2. **Keep the language level at A2**: short, clear, practical, everyday Latvian.
3. **Do not copy** sample tasks, texts, or exact wording from the source material.
4. Make every exam **original**, but structurally parallel to the official format.
5. Use **realistic Latvian names, places, dates, times, prices, and daily-life situations**.
6. Ensure that each task has **one clear correct answer** unless the task type explicitly allows multiple acceptable model answers.
7. Keep vocabulary and grammar within **basic everyday communication**.
8. Avoid B1/B2 complexity, abstract topics, long explanations, rare vocabulary, and tricky ambiguity.
9. Each mock exam must be **self-contained** and ready to save as a separate Markdown file.
10. Return the exams in **clean Markdown**.

## Global exam format

Each mock exam must contain the following four parts:

### 1. Klausīšanās prasmes pārbaude
- **Total:** 15 points
- **Time:** 25 minutes
- **Format:** 3 tasks

#### Task 1
- 6 short announcements
- For each announcement:
  - 1 question
  - 3 answer options
  - exactly 1 correct answer
- Difficulty: simple practical listening (transport, shop, weather, event, opening hours, etc.)

#### Task 2
- 1 dialogue
- 4 statements about the dialogue
- Learner marks **Jā / Nē**
- Exactly 4 items

#### Task 3
- 5 short dialogues
- 5 sentences with one missing word or number each
- 9 answer options total
- Exactly **4 distractors**
- Answers should be simple words or numbers

#### For listening section, you must also provide:
- **Answer key**
- **Full listening transcripts** for all listening tasks

---

### 2. Lasītprasmes pārbaude
- **Total:** 15 points
- **Time:** 30 minutes
- **Format:** 3 tasks

#### Task 1
- 4 short texts
- Each text about **80-110 words**
- Each text has **3 statements**
- Learner chooses the **1 correct statement** for each text

#### Task 2
- 6 short real-life situations/descriptions
- 12 short ads/notices
- Learner matches each situation to the correct ad/notice
- Ads should be practical and realistic:
  - apartment rental
  - pharmacy
  - hair salon
  - parcel delivery
  - car repair
  - events
  - bookstore
  - café
  - travel
  - classes
  - etc.

#### Task 3
- 1 text about **90-120 words**
- 5 blanks
- Each blank has **3 options**
- Exactly 1 correct option per blank

#### For reading section, you must also provide:
- **Answer key**

---

### 3. Rakstītprasmes pārbaude
- **Total:** 15 points
- **Time:** 35 minutes
- **Format:** 3 tasks

#### Task 1
- 4 picture-based prompts
- Since you may not generate actual images, provide **4 short image descriptions**
- The learner must write **1 sentence per image**
- Each sentence must contain **at least 5 words**

#### Task 2
- 5 sentences with a word in brackets
- The learner must write the word in the **correct form**
- Across the 5 items, use:
  - 1 noun
  - 1 verb
  - 1 pronoun
  - 1 adjective
  - 1 numeral
- Keep inflection demands appropriate for A2

#### Task 3
Create one short writing task of about **35 words**, based on one of these formats:
- SMS / īsziņa
- e-pasts
- WhatsApp ziņa
- sludinājums
- short personal message

Include **4 clear content points** the learner must cover.

#### For writing section, you must also provide:
- **Model answer(s)** or **sample acceptable answer**
- Brief **checking notes** for what must be present in the answer

---

### 4. Runātprasmes pārbaude
- **Total:** 15 points
- **Time:** 10-15 minutes
- **Format:** 3 tasks

#### Task 1 (warm-up / interview)
- 9-10 simple examiner questions
- Topics: personal information, work, study, shopping, free time, travel, family, daily life
- Questions must be answerable in short full sentences

#### Task 2
- 2 picture-based speaking prompts
- Since you may not generate real images, provide **2 short image descriptions**
- Include the guiding questions:
  - **Kas?**
  - **Ko dara?**
  - **Kur?**
- Add **1 personal experience question** connected to the theme
- This task should correspond to the main speaking performance task

#### Task 3
- 3 short notices/ads with one missing detail to ask about
- The learner must formulate **1 full question** for each notice
- Missing details should be things like:
  - date
  - time
  - floor
  - price
  - address
  - duration

#### For speaking section, you must also provide:
- **Sample acceptable questions/answers**
- A short **teacher key**

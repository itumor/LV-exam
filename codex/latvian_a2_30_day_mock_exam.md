# Latvian A2 Exam — 30-Day Study Plan + Mock Exam

Based on the uploaded VISC 2024 document: **Valsts valodas prasmes pārbaude, pamata (A2) līmenis**.

## Real exam format from the document

You must pass **each** skill separately.

| Part | Max | Pass minimum | Time | Format |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Klausīšanās / Listening | 15 | 9 | 25 min | computer |
| Lasīšana / Reading | 15 | 9 | 30 min | computer |
| Rakstīšana / Writing | 15 | 9 | 35 min | paper |
| Runāšana / Speaking | 15 | 9 | 10–15 min | interview / video or in person |

Total: **60 points**. But important: even if total is good, you need **at least 9/15 in every skill**.

# 30-Day Study Plan

Daily time target: **45–75 minutes**.  
Every day: speak Latvian out loud for at least **5–10 minutes**.

## Week 1 — Build exam basics

### Day 1 — Diagnostic
- Do the mock exam below without notes.
- Mark weak areas: listening, reading, writing, or speaking.
- Write down 20 words you did not know.

### Day 2 — Personal information + family
Vocabulary: vārds, uzvārds, adrese, tālrunis, dzimšanas datums, ģimene, sieva, vīrs, bērni, vecāki.  
Practice speaking:
- Kāds ir jūsu vārds un uzvārds?
- Kur jūs dzīvojat?
- Cik ilgi jūs dzīvojat Latvijā?
Writing: 5 sentences about yourself and your family.

### Day 3 — Home and city
Vocabulary: dzīvoklis, māja, istaba, virtuve, blakus, netālu, autobusa pietura, veikals.  
Task: describe your home in 8 simple sentences.
Speaking: explain what is near your home.

### Day 4 — Work and daily routine
Vocabulary: darbs, darbavieta, kolēģi, pienākumi, sākas, beidzas, darba diena.  
Grammar: present tense verbs: es strādāju, tu strādā, viņš strādā.  
Writing: describe your normal workday.

### Day 5 — Time, dates, weather
Practice: clock times, weekdays, months, seasons.  
Listening practice: ask someone / use TTS to read short announcements with times and dates.
Speaking: Kāds šodien ir laiks? Ko jūs darāt sestdien?

### Day 6 — Shopping + food
Vocabulary: veikals, tirgus, maize, piens, gaļa, dārzeņi, maksā, atlaide, kilograms, litrs.  
Role-play: buy food in a shop.
Writing: 35-word message about what you need to buy.

### Day 7 — Review + mini test
- 15 minutes vocabulary review.
- 15 minutes reading: one advertisement.
- 15 minutes writing: 5 sentences.
- 10 minutes speaking: answer 10 A2 questions.

## Week 2 — Services and real-life situations

### Day 8 — Café/restaurant
Practice ordering food and asking price.  
Writing: short message inviting a friend to a café.

### Day 9 — Health
Vocabulary: sāp galva/kakls/vēders, temperatūra, ārsts, aptieka, zāles.  
Role-play: make a doctor appointment.

### Day 10 — Transport
Vocabulary: autobuss, tramvajs, vilciens, pietura, biļete, maršruts, brauciens.  
Practice asking: Kā nokļūt līdz...? Cik maksā biļete?

### Day 11 — Post, bank, repairs
Vocabulary: pasts, sūtījums, banka, maksājums, remonts, salabot.  
Writing: advertisement/request for repair.

### Day 12 — Free time + hobbies
Vocabulary: lasīt, skatīties TV, sportot, ceļot, muzejs, kino, koncerts.  
Speaking: Ko jūs darāt brīvajā laikā? Kāpēc?

### Day 13 — Holidays and invitations
Vocabulary: dzimšanas diena, Ziemassvētki, ielūgums, dāvana, svinēt.  
Writing: invite a friend to a birthday party.

### Day 14 — Weekly mock speaking
Record yourself answering:
1. Pastāstiet par sevi.
2. Pastāstiet par savu darbu.
3. Kur jūs pērkat pārtiku?
4. Kā jūs atpūšaties?
5. Kur jūs ceļojāt?

## Week 3 — Grammar and exam task types

### Day 15 — Nouns and cases: basic patterns
Focus: uz skolu, veikalā, ar draugu, par darbu, man ir.  
Write 10 short sentences.

### Day 16 — Adjectives and agreement
Practice: jauns/jauna/jauni/jaunas; liels/liela; labs/laba.  
Write descriptions of objects: krēsls, soma, mašīna, dzīvoklis.

### Day 17 — Pronouns
Practice: mans, mana, mani, manas; savs, sava, saviem.  
Writing: 8 sentences about your things and your family.

### Day 18 — Numbers and prices
Practice: dates, ages, prices, time.  
Listening: write down numbers from short announcements.

### Day 19 — Past tense
Practice: biju, braucu, strādāju, nopirku, redzēju, patika.  
Writing: 35 words about a trip or weekend.

### Day 20 — Reading matching tasks
Practice matching situations to advertisements.  
Strategy: identify need first, then location/service/time/price.

### Day 21 — Full reading practice
Do the reading part of the mock exam again or create similar tasks.
Time limit: 30 minutes.

## Week 4 — Exam simulation and correction

### Day 22 — Listening focus
Use the listening scripts below. Read them aloud twice or use text-to-speech.  
Answer without reading the script.

### Day 23 — Writing focus: 4 image sentences
Practice 4 sentences, minimum 5 words each.  
Use: family, home, shopping, hobby.

### Day 24 — Writing focus: word forms
Practice adjective, noun, pronoun, verb, number forms.

### Day 25 — Writing focus: 35-word message/ad
Write one of these:
- message about a trip
- advertisement selling furniture
- invitation to a party
- message to doctor/repair service

### Day 26 — Speaking focus: interview
Answer 20 questions out loud. Use full sentences. Do not answer with one word.

### Day 27 — Speaking focus: picture description
For any picture, say:
- Kas ir attēlā?
- Ko cilvēki dara?
- Kur viņi atrodas?
- Kāda ir jūsu pieredze?

### Day 28 — Speaking focus: ask questions
Practice asking questions:
- Kad notiks...?
- Cikos sāksies...?
- Kur atrodas...?
- Cik maksā...?
- Kā var sazināties...?

### Day 29 — Full mock exam simulation
Do the whole mock exam in one sitting:
- Listening 25 min
- Reading 30 min
- Writing 35 min
- Speaking 10–15 min

### Day 30 — Final review
- Review all mistakes.
- Memorize 30 useful phrases.
- Prepare simple answers about yourself, work, family, shopping, free time, travel, health.
- Sleep well before the real exam.

# Scoring notes

## Writing
- Task 1: 1 point per clear sentence with at least 5 words.
- Task 2: 1 point per correct word form.
- Task 3: 6 points: answer all 4 points, clear meaning, simple correct sentences, around 35 words.

## Speaking
Aim for:
- full sentences, not one-word answers;
- simple but clear grammar;
- asking for repetition if needed: **Atkārtojiet, lūdzu!** / **Es nesapratu jautājumu.**

Useful phrases:
- Es dzīvoju Rīgā.
- Es strādāju par ...
- Brīvajā laikā man patīk ...
- Es parasti pērku pārtiku ...
- Man patika ceļojums, jo ...
- Man nepatika ..., jo ...
- Es domāju, ka ...
